ABOUT US

Oasis Cafe is a vintage retro-themed cafe that is always become a port for those who want a quiet and peaceful cafe atmosphere. This cafe is worth it for OOTD photos taking even from the outside it looks as usual but when you enter it, you will automatically be fascinated by its class.

The interior of this cafe is furnished with a touch of retro-ness like antique furniture and dim lights that will sweep you back to the past. Not only that, every dish and cup of coffee on the menu is a work of art, well worth the price, and delectably delicious.

When you are stressed and need a peaceful atmosphere, come to the Oasis Cafe
